HTML5 Board Games. Developed by Ronald Bett.

Rubiks Match 3

Play the classic match 3 game.

Tethreemino

Match three falling tetriminos to score.

Train Master

Create a path for the train to pass through.

Cuttoo

Create a path around opponent/empty pieces to capture them and earn points. Touch opponent's piece earn them a point.

Towall

Move your chains to the opposite wall. The chains must not break. You cannot sit on your opponent.

Eneza

Duplicate your pieces faster than the opponent. Cut off the opponent.

Cornered

Move black pieces to the top row. Cornered the opponent and take their piece.

Cardle

Wordle inspired card game. Use deduction to guess the hidden cards.

Ziggotopia

Match 3 pieces of the same color and clear! Otherwise the board zigzag upwards. Zuma inspired.
